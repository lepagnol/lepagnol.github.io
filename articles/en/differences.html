Our Differences

how our candidacy differs from the other's ?

August 2020 Jérôme & Caroline

By applying for this position, we wish to represent all employees, in France and abroad by co-constructing with you to give voice and value to your ideas.
This is why, to ensure an optimal flow of information (sharing of ideas and information dispatching), we are working on an apps (for smartphones) that will be accessible to all employee shareholders.
While waiting for its implementation (after the October election), if you wish to enhance the value of our candidacy,
do not hesitate to share your ambitions and ideas for the common wealth of employees and the bank, by emailing to us.
