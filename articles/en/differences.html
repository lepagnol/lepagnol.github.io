Quel est l'objectif de cette éléction ?

1 Aout 2020 Jerome & Caroline

By applying for this position, we wish to represent all employees, in France and abroad:
co-constructing to give voice and value to your ideas.
This is why, to ensure an optimal flow of information (sharing of ideas and information descent),
we are working on an application that will be accessible to all employee shareholders.
While waiting for its implementation (after the October election), if you wish to enhance the value of our candidacy,
do not hesitate to share your ambitions and ideas for the common good of employees and the bank, by email.
